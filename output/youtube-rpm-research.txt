YOUTUBE RPM/CPM RESEARCH BY NICHE (2025-2026)
Comprehensive Data-Driven Analysis for Faceless AI Channels
================================================================

IMPORTANT CONTEXT:
- RPM = Revenue Per Mille (what YOU earn per 1,000 views after YouTube's 45% cut)
- CPM = Cost Per Mille (what ADVERTISERS pay per 1,000 ad impressions)
- Rule of thumb: RPM is roughly 45-55% of CPM
- YouTube average CPM: ~$3.50 | Average RPM: ~$1.25-$2.50
- US/UK/Canada/Australia audiences earn 2-10x more than global averages
- Q4 (Oct-Dec) typically 30-50% higher than Q1-Q2

2. MEDITATION / GUIDED MEDITATION
--------------------------------------
RPM: $3-$7 (general meditation) | $10.92 (sleep/healing soundscapes — specific sub-niche)
CPM: $5-$12
Competition: HIGH for general meditation, MODERATE for specialized sub-niches
Growth rate: 5.4x for sleep soundscapes

REAL CHANNEL EXAMPLES:

- Jason Stephenson (Sleep Meditation Music): 3M subs, 900M views, ~$30K/mo

YouTube's July 2025 policy update specifically targets "looped audio" channels. If you loop the same 30-second rain sound for 8 hours across 50 videos, that's getting flagged as "inauthentic content." You MUST add genuine creative value — unique compositions, original guided scripts, varied visuals.

VERDICT: The $10.92 RPM for sleep/healing soundscapes is STRONG — but that number comes with a huge asterisk due to YouTube's crackdown on repetitive content. If you can create genuinely unique meditations with original scripts and compositions, this is viable. Pure ambient loops are dying.

3. SLEEP CONTENT (Rain Sounds, Sleep Stories, Ambient)
--------------------------------------
RPM: $10.92 (soundscapes/healing category) | $1.36-$3.40 (generic ambient/music)
CPM: $5-$15
Competition: MODERATE-HIGH (~20K channels in sleep/healing specifically)
Growth rate: 5.4x

REAL CHANNEL EXAMPLES:
- A single rain sounds video with 220M+ views = estimated $100K+ lifetime earnings
- One meditation channel with 145M views = estimated $190K-$500K lifetime revenue
- Sleep channels making "$10K/mo" with 8-10 hour videos (per Medium articles — take with grain of salt)

AUDIENCE: 20-50, slight female skew, heavy US/UK traffic (people searching before bed = high-value evening ad slots)
BEST FORMAT: 8-10 hour videos are the play — people leave them running all night = massive watch time = algorithm boost + more mid-roll ads. Also 30-60 min "sleep stories" with narrative.
AI/FACELESS SUITABILITY: PERFECT — literally nobody shows their face in this niche

KEY ADVANTAGE: People REWATCH these videos every single night. One video can generate views for YEARS.
KEY RISK: The $10.92 RPM is the BEST case. Many ambient channels report much lower ($1.36-$3.40) because:
  - Global audience (India, Southeast Asia = low RPM)
  - YouTube may classify as "music" category (lowest CPMs at $1.36 avg)
  - Repetitive content = potential demonetization under July 2025 rules

VERDICT: High ceiling ($10+ RPM) but wide variance. The difference between $1.36 and $10.92 RPM comes down to: (a) audience geography, (b) whether YouTube classifies you as "music" vs "health/wellness", (c) content originality. Sleep STORIES with narrative structure will outperform pure ambient loops.

--------------------------------------
RPM: $3-$7 (estimated based on overlap with motivation/wellness categories)
CPM: $5-$10 (categorized under "Motivational & Spiritual" at $5-$10 CPM)

DEMONETIZATION RISK: MODERATE — if it's just text scrolling with a robotic TTS voice, YouTube will flag it. Add genuine production value (custom music, thoughtful visual design, original scripts).

VERDICT: Moderate RPM but INSANE repeat viewership makes up for it. A channel with 100 well-made affirmation videos could generate consistent daily income. The lack of specific RPM data for this niche is itself a finding — it means fewer people are doing it well enough to report earnings, which = opportunity.

================================================================
CRITICAL WARNING: YOUTUBE AI DEMONETIZATION (July 2025)
================================================================

On July 15, 2025, YouTube renamed "repetitious content" as "inauthentic content" and is actively cracking down on:

RED FLAGS (will get demonetized):
- Same 30-second audio loop extended to 8 hours
- AI text-to-speech with no human editing/personality
- Stock footage slideshows with recycled scripts
- Batch-uploaded near-identical videos
- Robotic cadence and repeated templates

SAFE ZONE (still monetizable):
- AI as a TOOL in the creative process (not the entire process)
- Original scripts with genuine insights
- Human-edited voiceovers (even if AI-assisted)
- Unique visual compositions
- Commentary, storytelling, or educational value added

COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS:
- Must disclose synthetic content using YouTube's AI disclosure toggle
- Must add meaningful commentary, editing, or transformative input
- Must demonstrate genuine creative contribution

Bottom line: "Use AI to assist, not replace, the creative process."
