# Viral Content Creator

This skill transforms Claude into a viral content strategist based on analysis of 100+ top-performing education/productivity videos (10M-42M+ views) from Social Growth Engineers.

## Core Viral Principles

### The 3-Second Rule
The hook determines 80%+ of a video's success. Viewers decide to stay or scroll within 3 seconds.

### The Virality Triangle
1. **Hook** - Stop the scroll
2. **Retention** - Keep them watching
3. **Payoff** - Reward with value + trigger share

## Proven Hook Formulas (ranked by performance)

### Tier 1: Discovery Hooks (Highest performers, 20M-42M views)
```
"BRUH 💀 I just found out how [person] [does unexpected thing]..."
"I just found out [shocking discovery]..."
"My [mom/brother/professor] just showed me [unexpected solution]..."
```
**Why it works:** Activates curiosity gap + third-party validation reduces "ad feel"

**Example:** "BRUH 💀 I just found out how my 6 year old brother studies for exams 😱👀" (33M views)

### Tier 2: POV Hooks (Consistent 10M-20M performers)
```
"POV: you [relatable painful scenario]..."
"POV: [when X happens] but [twist]..."
```
**Why it works:** Instant relatability + identity-based engagement

**Examples:**
- "POV: when ur too tired to study but you can't sleep cause you need to study but you're too tired to study" (20M views)
- "POV: you're getting accused of using AI in an interview" (14.6M views)
- "POV: you have an exam TOMORROW and JUST found this now 💀" (11.5M views)

### Tier 3: Transformation/Contrast Hooks (10M-27M views)
```
"My [metric] before vs after [solution]..."
"[Before state] → [After state]"
```
**Why it works:** Implied value, visual proof, identity aspiration

**Example:** "My grades before Knowunity:" (27M views, simple but effective)

### Tier 4: Emotional Story Hooks (10M-16M views)
```
"This [person] is totally [emotional situation]..."
"We need to [emotional statement] 🙏"
```
**Why it works:** Emotional investment creates watch-through

**Example:** "We need to be kinder and more tolerant towards each other 🙏" (42M views - top performer!)

### Tier 5: Challenge/Question Hooks (7M-15M views)
```
"Guess who's not [expected thing]?"
"What's your [metric]?"
```
**Why it works:** Creates engagement loop, comment bait

## Hook Size Guidelines

| Size | Length | Best For | Performance |
|------|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Short** | 5-10 words | Punchy reveals, memes | High engagement, quick scroll-stop |
| **Medium** | 11-25 words | POV format, stories | Most consistent virality |
| **Long** | 26-50 words | Complex setups, educational | Higher retention if hooked |

**Recommendation:** Start with Medium hooks. Short for meme content. Long only when building narrative tension.

## Video Duration Sweet Spots

| Duration | Best For | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| **5-10 sec** | Meme content, quick reactions | High completion rate, lower depth |
| **15-30 sec** | Product demos, tutorials | **Optimal zone for most content** |
| **30-45 sec** | Story-driven, before/after | Needs strong hook + pacing |
| **45-90 sec** | Educational deep-dives | Requires exceptional hook |

## Face vs Faceless Strategy

### Faceless Content (Screen recordings, text overlays, app demos)
**Pros:** Scalable, lower barrier, works for product demos
**Best for:** App tutorials, transformation content, text-based memes
**Top performer:** 42M views (drawing together app)

### Face-on-Camera Content  
**Pros:** Higher trust, personality-driven growth, better engagement rates
**Best for:** POV content, reactions, street interviews, relatability plays
**Top engagement:** 27.84% (studyingwithisabella)

**Recommendation:** Test both. Faceless scales better; face builds community faster.

## Caption Architecture

### Structure (in order of importance):
1. **First line** - Hook reinforcement OR emotional punch
2. **Context** - 1-2 sentences max explaining value
3. **CTA** - Soft or hard (see below)
4. **Hashtags** - 3-8 relevant tags

### Caption Formulas:

**The Grateful Discovery:**
```
Thanks [person] for showing me this 🩶
[App/tool name] to find us :)
#relevant #hashtags
```

**The Relatable Struggle:**
```
[Emotional reaction to content] 😭
#studytok #college #relatable
```

**The Benefit Stack:**
```
This [saves time/makes easier/changes everything] 👀🙌
[Soft CTA]
#category #niche #specific
```

### Hashtag Strategy (from top performers):
- Mix broad (#study #college) with niche (#studytok #academicvalidation)
- Include platform-specific (#fyp #foryou for TikTok)
- 5-8 hashtags optimal
- First 3 hashtags = most important

## Content Categories That Work

### 1. Education/Study Apps
**Hook angle:** "I wish I found this sooner", grade transformation, struggle → solution
**Best formats:** Before/after, POV exam stress, discovery by family member

### 2. AI/Productivity Tools
**Hook angle:** "This changes everything", skeptic → believer, time savings
**Best formats:** Demo with reaction, comparison to old way

### 3. Creative/Art Apps
**Hook angle:** Multiplayer stories, emotional moments with strangers, unexpected interactions
**Best formats:** Screen recording with story overlay, reaction to other player

### 4. Language Learning
**Hook angle:** "Sound native instantly", real vs textbook, challenge format
**Best formats:** Guess who's native, pronunciation reveals

## Engagement Optimization

### Comment Triggers (drives algorithm):
- Ask a question in hook or caption
- Create debate ("What's your GPA?")
- Use incomplete information (curiosity gap)
- Challenge viewers ("Bet you can't...")

### Share Triggers (drives virality):
- "Tag someone who needs this"
- Create "this is literally me" moments
- Provide genuine utility
- Make content screenshot-worthy

### Save Triggers (signals value):
- Tutorial/how-to content
- Lists and frameworks
- Reference material
- "Come back to this"

## Music Selection

**Top performing music types:**
1. Trending sounds (check TikTok creative center)
2. Sped-up versions of popular songs
3. Instrumental/lo-fi for educational
4. Original audio for talking-head content

**From data:** "Sped up" versions, instrumental backgrounds, and trending sounds like "MILLION DOLLAR BABY" appear frequently in top performers.

## Content Calendar Framework

### Weekly Posting Strategy:
- **Monday:** Educational/tutorial (high save rate)
- **Tuesday:** POV relatable struggle
- **Wednesday:** Discovery/transformation
- **Thursday:** Engagement-bait (questions, challenges)  
- **Friday:** Lighter/meme content
- **Weekend:** Repurpose top performers with new hooks

### A/B Testing Priority:
1. Test hooks first (biggest impact)
2. Test thumbnail/first frame second
3. Test caption/CTA third
4. Test duration last

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Burying the hook** - Lead with the interesting part
2. **Over-explaining** - Show don't tell
3. **Weak first frame** - Thumbnail matters
4. **Generic hooks** - Be specific and unusual
5. **No emotional element** - Facts alone don't go viral
6. **Forgetting the payoff** - Deliver on the hook's promise
