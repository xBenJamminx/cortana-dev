## Outlier Rate Explained

The data includes "Outlier Rate" - how much a video outperformed the account average:
- **>1000x:** Viral breakout (e.g., studyfetchwilliam at 24,861x)
- **>100x:** Major hit
- **>10x:** Strong performer
- **1-10x:** Normal range

**Key insight:** Even established accounts have viral outliers, suggesting hook quality > account size for individual video performance.
