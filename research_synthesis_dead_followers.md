### 2.2 YouTube: Video-Centric Evaluation

**Key Finding:** YouTube explicitly denies any penalty for inactive subscribers, evaluating each video independently rather than channel-wide metrics (Claude, ChatGPT).

#### Official YouTube Position
Todd Beaupré, Senior Director of Growth & Discovery: *"Inactive subscribers will not impact overall distribution"* (ChatGPT, Claude)

#### Algorithm Mechanics
- **99.7%** of watch time comes from non-subscribers via browse/suggested features (ChatGPT)
- Only **0.3%** from subscriber notifications (ChatGPT)
- Algorithm evaluates **each video independently** rather than penalizing channel-wide metrics (Claude)
- No "subscriber quality" metric in YouTube's algorithm (ChatGPT)

---

### 2.3 TikTok: Content-First Architecture

**Key Finding:** TikTok operates almost entirely on content quality signals, making follower engagement largely irrelevant to reach (Claude, ChatGPT).

#### Algorithm Mechanics
- **90-95%** of videos seen by users selected by recommendation algorithms, not following relationships (ChatGPT, Claude)
- Follower count and previous video performance are **"not direct factors"** in For You Page recommendations (ChatGPT)
- Algorithm evaluates: watch completion (**8 points**), rewatch/loop (**10 points**), shares (**6 points**), comments (**4 points**) (ChatGPT)
