# AI Tool Affiliate & Partner Programs Research

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-07

---

## Priority Tools (Stack & Workflow)

### 1. Composio (composio.dev)

**Affiliate Program:** No public affiliate program found.

**Startups Program:**
- $2,000 in usage credits once in production
- Eligible startups can get up to $25K in free credits
- 1:1 priority support via shared Slack channel
- Custom app/integration requests fulfilled
- Eligibility: Must have raised no more than Series A
- Apply: https://forms.composio.dev/startups

**Partner Program:** Integration partners can list in the Composio marketplace. No referral commissions disclosed.

**Verdict:** No affiliate revenue opportunity. Startup credits are the main benefit.

---

### 2. ElevenLabs (elevenlabs.io)

**Affiliate Program:** YES -- active and well-structured.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Commission | 22% recurring for 12 months (Starter/Creator/Pro/Scale plans) |
| Business Plan | 11% commission |
| Enterprise | No commissions |
| Cookie Duration | 90 days |
| Payout Delay | 90-day activation period before first payout |
| Payment Schedule | 1st-15th of each month |
| Min Payout | $5 |
| Platform | PartnerStack |

**How to Apply:** https://elevenlabs.io/affiliates

**Guide:** https://elevenlabs.io/affiliate-partner-guide

**Terms:** https://elevenlabs.io/affiliates-terms

**Verdict:** Strong program. 22% recurring for a year on a tool people actually keep paying for. High conversion potential given the product quality.

### 3. Vercel (vercel.com)

**Partner Program:** YES -- agency/consultant focused.
- Enterprise sandbox access
- Enablement and priority support
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Expert listing in marketplace
- Referral pipeline
- Apply: https://vercel.com/partners

**v0 Ambassador Program:**
- $100/month in v0 credits
- Early access to new features
- Limited-edition v0 swag
- Personal referral link (commission on subscriptions)
- Access to v0 micro-grants
- Limited spots, selective approval
- Apply: https://v0.app/ambassador

**Affiliate Terms:** https://vercel.com/legal/affiliate-marketing-terms

**Startups Program:** https://vercel.com/startups

**Verdict:** The v0 Ambassador program is the best entry point. Commission rates not publicly disclosed but the program exists. Agency partner program is more for consulting shops.

---

### 4. Clerk (clerk.com)

**Affiliate Program:** No traditional affiliate program.

**Creator Partnership Program:**
- Performance-based CPM model: $20-$55 per 1,000 video views
- Full creative freedom, no deadlines
- Dedicated support channel
- 60% of partnerships go to up-and-coming creators
- 40% to established creators
- Apply: https://clerk.com/creators (click "Request Partnership")

**Startups Program:** https://clerk.com/startups

**Verdict:** Not a standard affiliate program. More of a sponsored content / creator deal. Good if you're making dev tutorial videos. CPM-based, not conversion-based.

---

### 5. Supabase (supabase.com)

**Affiliate Program:** No public affiliate program.

**Partner Program (Integration):**
- List your integration in the Partners marketplace
- Requires official business registration and meaningful revenue or VC backing
- Apply: https://forms.supabase.com/partner

**SupaSquad Advocate Program:**
- Community advocate role (not paid commissions)
- Benefits: Discord badge, early feature access, direct team access, exclusive discounts on dev tools, merch, monthly AMA sessions with DevRel team
- Growth path: volunteer -> paid contributor -> employee
- Apply: https://supabase.com/supasquad

**Verdict:** No affiliate revenue. SupaSquad is a community play, not a money play. Integration partner program is for product companies, not individual affiliates.

**Affiliate Program:** YES -- strong program.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Commission | 30% recurring for 12 months |
| Eligible Plans | n8n Cloud only (Starter, Pro, Enterprise) |
| Cookie Duration | 30-90 days (conflicting reports) |
| Payment | Via PartnerStack dashboard |

**How to Apply:** https://n8n.io/affiliates/ or https://dash.partnerstack.com/application?company=n8n&group=affiliates2025

**Expert Partners Program:** Being revamped, launching late 2025/early 2026.

### 7. Make.com (make.com)

**Affiliate Program:** YES -- highest commission in the automation space.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Commission | 35% recurring for 12 months |
| Cookie Duration | Not publicly stated |
| Min Customers | 3 unique customers before first payout |
| Min Payout | $100 |
| Payment Method | PayPal.me only |
| Eligible Revenue | Subscription payments only (not extra operations) |
| Commission Window | 12 months from user registration date |

**How to Apply:** https://www.make.com/en/affiliate

**Partner Program (agencies):** https://www.make.com/en/partners

**Help Center:** https://help.make.com/affiliate-program

**Verdict:** Best automation affiliate deal available. 35% for 12 months beats n8n (30%) and Zapier (nothing). PayPal-only payment is the main downside.

### 8. Cursor (cursor.com)

**Affiliate Program:** NO -- does not exist.

**Referral Program:** Has a referral URL structure (cursor.com/en/referral) but no formal program with commissions. Community has requested it multiple times on forums.

**Verdict:** No revenue opportunity. Monitor for future program launch.

### 9. Claude / Anthropic (anthropic.com)

**Enterprise Referral Partner Program:**
- One-time fee per referred customer (amount not publicly disclosed)
- Must refer customers outside Anthropic's existing sales territories
- Deals need to be substantial (Fortune 2000-level companies)
- Not recurring -- single payment per deal
- Terms effective March 26, 2025
- Apply: https://anthropic.com/referral/

**VC Partner Program:**
- For VCs and accelerators
- Provides API credits to portfolio companies
- Exclusive events and product updates
- Apply: https://claude.com/contact-sales/vc-partner

**Development Partner Program:**
- Opt-in data sharing for model improvement
- Not a revenue program
- Details: https://support.claude.com/en/articles/11174108-about-the-development-partner-program

**Verdict:** Enterprise referral program is for big-deal door openers, not content creators or community builders. Not relevant for individual affiliate income.
