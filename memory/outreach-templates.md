# Outreach Templates — Parker & Taylor + OpenConcierge
**Updated: Feb 16, 2026**

## Principles
- Consultative, not salesy
- Reference specific signal (hiring post, growth event, overwhelm)
- No pitch deck in first touch
- Short — respect their time
- Position as peer/operator, not vendor
- Follow-up cadence: Day 0 → Day 3 → Day 7 → Day 14 → Park

---

## PARKER & TAYLOR TEMPLATES

### Lane A: Hiring Signal Interception

#### A1 — Ops Manager / Implementation Hire
```
Hey [Name],

Saw you're hiring for [role] — congrats on the growth. That's usually a sign things are scaling faster than the current team can absorb.

Before you commit to the full-time headcount, might be worth a quick conversation about whether some of that operational load could be structurally automated instead. I work with lean operator teams on exactly this — typically reduces 10-20 hours/week of manual process work.

Not pitching a replacement for the hire — just a diagnostic conversation. Happy to share a few examples if useful.

Ben
```

#### A2 — RevOps / Sales Ops Hire
```
Hey [Name],

Noticed you're bringing on a [RevOps/Sales Ops] hire. That usually means pipeline or reporting workflows have outgrown the current setup.

I help founder-led teams build automation infrastructure for exactly those bottlenecks — CRM workflows, reporting pipelines, client onboarding sequences. Most projects are 30-day fixed scope — we identify the highest-impact bottleneck and build the solution.

Would a 15-minute diagnostic call be useful before you finalize the hire? Sometimes a few targeted automations change the headcount math.

Ben
```

## LOOM SCRIPT (post-engagement, 60-90 sec)
1. "Hey [Name], wanted to put a face to the name..." (10 sec)
2. Quick context on what I do (15 sec)
3. Specific observation about their business (20 sec)
4. One concrete example of what I've automated for similar teams (30 sec)
5. Soft CTA — "Would love to explore if any of this applies to you" (10 sec)

---

## POST-CALL: ONE-PAGE PROPOSAL FRAMEWORK
1. **Context** — What we discussed, what's broken
2. **Recommendation** — 30-Day Build or Ongoing Partnership
3. **Scope** — Specific workflows/systems to build
4. **Timeline** — 30 days for build, 3-month min for partnership
5. **Investment** — $6k-$8k build / $4k-$6k/mo partnership
6. **Next Step** — Sign-off + access collection + onboarding checklist
