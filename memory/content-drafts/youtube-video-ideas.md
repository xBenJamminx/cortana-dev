# YouTube Video Ideas — AI Content Pivot

## Strategy Notes
- 8K existing subs gives you a distribution head start
- AI content is universally interesting — most of your existing audience will engage
- Keep videos 8-15 min (sweet spot for YouTube algo + retention)
- Format: Screen share + face cam (no fancy production needed)
- Post 2x/month to start, increase if it gains traction

---

## Video Format Template

```
0:00 - Hook (15 sec) — State the result/promise
0:15 - Context (45 sec) — Why this matters, who it's for
1:00 - The Framework/Demo (6-10 min) — Core content, screen share
~10:00 - Key Takeaway (30 sec) — One sentence summary
~10:30 - CTA (30 sec) — Subscribe, newsletter link, next video tease
```

---

## Thumbnail Style
- Face + emotion (surprised, thinking)
- Bold text overlay (3-5 words max)
- Bright colors (yellow/red work best for AI content)
- Examples:
  - "I Quit 90% of AI Tools" + surprised face
  - "$500 PROMPT" + pointing at screen
  - "2 HOUR Analysis" + timer graphic
