# Cross-Platform Content Strategy

## The System: One Idea, Five Platforms

The goal is NEVER creating 5 pieces of content. It's creating 1 piece of content and adapting it 5 ways.

---

## Platform Hierarchy

### Tier 1: PRIMARY (post here first)
**LinkedIn** — Daily text posts, Mon-Fri
- Your B2B audience lives here
- Investment firm partners, founders, business owners
- Algorithm rewards: text posts, dwell time, comments
- Best times: Tue-Thu, 8-10am ET

### Tier 2: OWNED (weekly, builds the moat)
**Substack** — Weekly newsletter, Tuesday 8am ET
- 227 existing subs = head start
- You OWN this audience (no algorithm, no suspension risk)
- Deeper dives on LinkedIn topics
- Growth channel: LinkedIn → Substack funnel

### Tier 3: VISUAL (bi-weekly, expands reach)
**YouTube** — 2x/month, tutorial/walkthrough style
- 8K existing subs = distribution advantage
- AI tutorials, tool walkthroughs, "how I built this" content
- Long-tail SEO value (videos rank for years)
- Repurpose: Clips → TikTok, LinkedIn video

### Tier 4: SHORT-FORM (repurpose, low effort)
**TikTok (AI account)** — 3-5x/week, repurposed clips
- YouTube clips chopped into 30-60 sec shorts
- Quick tips, "one prompt that changed X"
- Building from 0 so expectations are low — just be consistent

### Tier 5: COMMUNITY (engagement, not content)
**Telegram/Discord AI chats** — Daily participation
- Share your LinkedIn posts
- Answer questions (positions you as expert)
- Not a content creation channel — a distribution channel

## Weekly Content Workflow

### Sunday (30 min): PLAN
- Pick the week's core topic (from content calendar)
- Outline the Substack deep dive
- Draft 5 LinkedIn post angles from the same topic

### Monday (45 min): CREATE
- Write the Substack newsletter (publish Tuesday)
- Write Monday + Tuesday LinkedIn posts

### Tuesday (15 min): PUBLISH + ENGAGE
- Substack goes out at 8am ET
- LinkedIn post goes up at 9am ET
- Spend 15 min commenting on others' posts

### Wednesday-Friday (15 min/day): PUBLISH + ENGAGE
- Post pre-written LinkedIn content
- Reply to all comments
- 15 min engaging on others' content

### Bi-weekly Saturday (2 hours): VIDEO
- Record YouTube video on the week's topic
- Keep it simple: screen share + face cam
- Edit with AI tools (Descript or CapCut)
- Post Monday of the following week

## Content Repurposing Chain

```
CORE IDEA (e.g., "Prompt Engineering Basics")
    │
    ├── LinkedIn Post (Day 2): Short framework post (300 words)
    │
    ├── Substack Issue: Full deep dive with examples (1,500 words)
    │
    ├── YouTube Video: "3 Prompt Techniques" tutorial (8-12 min)
    │   │
    │   ├── TikTok Clip 1: Persona Priming technique (45 sec)
    │   ├── TikTok Clip 2: Chain-of-thought technique (45 sec)
    │   └── TikTok Clip 3: Best tip (30 sec)
    │
    └── Community Share: Post LinkedIn link + "what prompts do you use?"
```

**One idea = 7+ pieces of content across 5 platforms**

## Content Pillars (Rotating Weekly)

1. **Prompt Engineering** — Techniques, frameworks, before/after examples
2. **AI Tools & Stack** — What to use, what to skip, real cost analysis
3. **AI for Business** — Proposals, emails, competitive analysis, operations
4. **Building & Launching** — Side projects, MVPs, validation, pricing
5. **AI Life Hacks** — Personal productivity, finances, planning (shows human side)

Rotate through these. Each pillar maps to existing content in the vault.

## KPIs to Track

### LinkedIn (Monthly)
- Followers: Target +200/month for first 3 months
- Avg impressions per post: Target 1,000+ by month 2
- Engagement rate: Target 4%+ (above platform avg)
- Profile views: Track week over week
- Connection requests received: Track inbound interest

### Substack (Monthly)
- Subscribers: 227 → 500 (month 1), 500 → 1,000 (month 3)
- Open rate: Target 40%+ (newsletter avg is 20%)
- Click rate: Target 5%+

### YouTube (Monthly)
- Views per video: Establish baseline with first 4 videos
- Watch time: Target 50%+ average retention
- Subscribers from AI content: Track new vs existing

### TikTok (Monthly)
- Followers: Track from 0
- Views per video: Establish baseline
- Don't optimize too hard here — it's a repurpose channel

## Tools Needed

- **LinkedIn**: Native posting (free)
- **Substack**: Already have account (free)
- **YouTube**: Already have channel + auth (free)
- **TikTok**: Phone app (free)
- **Scheduling**: Could use Buffer free tier (3 channels, 10 posts/channel)
- **Graphics**: Canva free for carousel templates
- **Video editing**: CapCut (free) or Descript ($24/mo if serious about video)

**Total additional cost: $0-24/month**
