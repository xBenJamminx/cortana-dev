# AI Services Client Proposal Template

**By Ben | BuildsByBen**

---

Copy this template for every new client opportunity. Fill in the brackets, adjust the scope to match what you discussed on the discovery call, and send it as a PDF or clean doc. This template has closed real deals.

---

# Proposal: [PROJECT NAME]

**Prepared for:** [Client Name / Company]
**Prepared by:** [Your Name]
**Date:** [Date]
**Valid for:** 14 days

---

## The Problem

[2-3 sentences describing the specific problem the client told you about on the discovery call. Use THEIR words, not yours. If they said "we waste 10 hours a week on reports," write that exact phrase.]

Example:
> Your team currently spends 8-10 hours per week manually compiling sales reports from three different tools. By the time the report is done, the data is already a day old, and the format changes every time depending on who builds it.

---

## The Solution

[2-3 sentences describing what you'll build/deliver. Keep it simple. No jargon.]

Example:
> I'll build an automated reporting system that pulls data from [Tool A], [Tool B], and [Tool C], generates a standardized weekly report, and delivers it to your inbox every Monday at 8am. No manual work required.

### AI Prompt to Customize This Fast

After your discovery call, paste your notes into Claude or ChatGPT with this:

```
I just had a discovery call with a potential client. Here are my notes:

[PASTE YOUR CALL NOTES]

Using this proposal template structure, help me fill in:
1. "The Problem" section — use the client's own words
2. "The Solution" section — keep it simple, no jargon
3. "Deliverables" table — based on what we discussed
4. "Timeline" — realistic estimate
5. "Why Me" bullets — based on my background: [YOUR BACKGROUND]

Write in a professional but warm tone. No corporate speak.
Don't oversell. Be honest about what's included and what's not.
```
