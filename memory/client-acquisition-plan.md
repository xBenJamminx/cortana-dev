## CHANNEL 1: Cold Email via Apollo
**Why:** Highest volume, lowest time per touch. Runs on autopilot once set up. Apollo handles everything: lead sourcing, contact data, email sequences, AND domain warmup.
**Cost:** $49/mo (Basic plan, 30,000 credits/year, unlimited sequences, built-in warmup)
**Setup time:** 2-3 hours one-time, then 30 min/week to review replies.

### Setup Checklist
- [ ] Sign up for Apollo Basic ($49/mo) or start the 14-day free trial
- [ ] Buy a sending domain (NOT benjoselson.com, use something like parkerandtaylor.co)
- [ ] Connect the sending domain to Apollo and START WARMUP immediately (2-week bottleneck)
- [ ] Build your 3 target lists (see below)
- [ ] Load email sequences into Apollo

### Target Lists to Build in Apollo
Build 3 separate lists (200-300 contacts each to start):

**List 1: Hiring Signal**
- Filter: Companies hiring for "Operations Manager," "RevOps," "Implementation," "Executive Assistant"
- Company size: 5-50 employees
- Industry: Professional services, real estate, financial services, agencies
- Location: US (or your metro area for local play)
- Why: If they're hiring ops, they have ops pain. Your automation is cheaper + faster than a hire.

**List 2: Founder-Led Services Companies**
- Filter: CEO/Founder titles at companies with 5-30 employees
- Industry: Consulting, real estate, law, financial advisory, marketing agencies
- Revenue: $1M-$20M
- Why: These people personally feel the ops burden. They're your buyer.

**List 3: Growth Signal**
- Filter: Companies with recent funding, acquisitions, or headcount growth (Apollo tracks this)
- Company size: 10-50 employees
- Why: Growth = new operational complexity. Perfect timing for your offer.

### Email Sequences (3 emails per sequence, loaded directly in Apollo)

**Sequence 1: Hiring Intercept**
```
Subject: Quick thought on your [Role] hire

Hey {firstName},

Saw you're looking for a {jobTitle}. Before you commit to the full-time cost, worth knowing: most of the manual ops work that role would handle can be automated in about 30 days.

I recently did this for an investment firm. Cut 10+ hours/week of manual reporting and deal flow processing. No new hire needed.

Not saying skip the hire entirely. Just might change the scope (and salary) you need.

Worth a 15-minute call to see if any of that applies?

Ben
```

**Email 2 (Day 3):**
```
Subject: Re: Quick thought on your {jobTitle} hire

Hey {firstName}, just bumping this up. Happy to share a quick example of what "automating the ops bottleneck" actually looks like in practice. No pitch, just context. 15 minutes?
```

**Email 3 (Day 7):**
```
Subject: Last one from me

{firstName}, I'll keep it short. If operational overhead ever becomes a priority, I help lean teams automate the manual stuff instead of adding headcount. Standing offer for a quick diagnostic chat whenever the timing works.

Ben
```

**Sequence 2: Founder Outreach**
```
Subject: Saving {companyName} 10+ hrs/week

Hey {firstName},

Running a {employeeCount}-person team means you're probably doing a lot of operational work yourself. Reporting, CRM updates, client onboarding, follow-ups.

I help founder-led companies automate those workflows. Not the "AI will change everything" pitch. Practical stuff: I identify your biggest time sink and build the automation in 30 days.

Recently did this for an investment firm. They went from 15+ hours/week of manual ops to under 3.

Would a 15-min diagnostic call be useful? I'll tell you exactly what's automatable and what's not.

Ben
```

### Volume Targets (once domain is warmed)
- Week 1-2: Domain warming via Apollo (no cold sends yet)
- Week 3: 25 emails/day across sequences
- Week 4+: 50 emails/day
- Expected reply rate: 3-5% (industry average for good cold email)
- At 50/day = 250/week = 8-12 replies/week = 2-4 calls/week

## CHANNEL 2: LinkedIn Outbound (Manual, High-Touch)
**Why:** Warmer than email. People see your face, your content, your profile. Higher conversion per touch.
**Cost:** Free
**Time: 30-45 min/day.**

### Daily Routine (Non-Negotiable)
1. **15 min:** Comment on 5-10 posts from target accounts (founders, operators, AI-adjacent people). Thoughtful comments, not "great post!" This is how you get on their radar BEFORE you DM them.
2. **15 min:** Send 5-10 connection requests with a note. Use the templates from outreach-templates.md (Lane A and B).
3. **15 min:** Follow up with existing connections who accepted but haven't replied.

### Weekly Targets
- 50 new connection requests/week (with personalized notes)
- 25 follow-up messages/week
- 10 meaningful comments/day on target accounts' posts
- Goal: 3-5 real conversations/week, 1-2 calls booked/week

### Profile Optimization (One-Time, Do This First)
- [ ] Headline: "I help lean teams automate ops instead of hiring. Founder @ Parker & Taylor."
- [ ] Banner: Simple, clean. "AI automation for operators" or similar.
- [ ] About section: Problem > solution > proof > CTA. Reference Five Points results (anonymized).
- [ ] Featured section: Pin your best post + a case study or proposal framework
- [ ] Turn on Creator Mode

**Why:** Other people sell for you. One relationship = multiple referrals.
**Cost:** Free
**Time: 2-3 hours this week, then 30 min/week maintenance.**

### Agency Partnership Outreach
Identify 10 people in these categories:

- Accountants / bookkeepers who serve SMBs

- HR consultants

No commitment, just putting it out there. Want to hop on a quick call so I can explain what I do?

Ben
