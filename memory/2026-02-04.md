## Ben's Content & Business Strategy (Alex Finn Model)

### North Star
Following Alex Finn's model explicitly:
- Build real things
- Investigate the systems around them
- Extract rules and insights
- Teach those insights publicly for free
- Convert trust into monetization later

**Not hype. Not shortcuts. Not "community first."**

### The Five Pillars

**1. Content & Personal Brand (FOUNDATIONAL)**
- Daily or near-daily public content
- Primarily X, then YouTube
- Mix: build-in-public, discoveries, rules people can apply, system-level explanations
- Not just sharing what YOU do → sharing what OTHERS can learn and reuse

**2. Building in Public (CREDIBILITY)**
- Retell (flagship)
- Real projects, client work, automations
- Done in the open = authority for teaching

**3. Discovery → Teaching (THE DIFFERENTIATOR)**
- Build → observe → extract rules → teach clearly
- What actually works vs looks cool
- Patterns others can apply immediately
- THIS is the Alex Finn move

**4. Products & Monetization (SUPPORTIVE, NOT DOMINANT)**
- Opportunistic, byproducts of work
- Reduce financial pressure, create leverage
- NOT constant launches, NOT center of attention

**5. Community (AMPLIFIER, LATER)**
- No free/paid community yet
- No Discord/Skool/Whop until pulled by demand
- Community converts belief into proximity AFTER leverage exists

### Financial Reality
- Currently cash-flow negative
- Leverage matters, obligation is dangerous, focus is critical
- Sequencing matters - pressure informs decisions, doesn't hijack them

### Order of Operations
1. Daily public value (build → discover → teach)
2. Retell as flagship proof
3. Selective monetization when something crystallizes
4. Simple capture (email, waitlist, interest signals)
5. Community ONLY when demanded

### What NOT to Do
- Don't rush community
- Don't optimize for engagement over substance
- Don't build courses before clarity
- Don't create obligations you can't sustain
- Don't copy Alex's surface tactics without his sequence
