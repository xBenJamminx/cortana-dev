## 8. DESIGN & COPY PATTERNS WORTH STEALING

### Copy Patterns
1. **Meta description formula:** "[Outcome-focused statement]. Join to [action], [action], and [action]."
2. **Plus headline formula:** "Learn to [mindset shift], [skill], and [outcome]."
3. **Template presentation:** Lead with 3 specific, tangible use cases (not vague categories)
4. **Audience segmentation:** Three clear audience buckets on landing page, each with their pain point
5. **CTA language:** Action verbs + benefit ("Claim Discount & Start Building" not "Sign Up")
6. **Pricing framing:** Show effective monthly cost on annual (makes annual look like a deal)

### Structural Patterns
1. **Freemium funnel:** Free community is MASSIVE (239K) -- the paid tier is tiny (3.1K) -- 1.3% conversion rate but at $94/mo that's ~$291K/mo MRR
2. **Course gating by tier:** Best courses locked behind annual -- pushes commitment
3. **Price lock as loyalty mechanism:** Once you join, you never want to cancel (you lose your rate)
4. **Weekly wins cadence:** Regular proof of results keeps community engaged and drives upgrades
5. **External landing page:** Separate from Skool about page -- purpose-built for cold traffic with discount offers
6. **Template as lead magnet:** 100+ templates = massive perceived value, zero marginal cost

### Urgency/Scarcity Playbook
1. Member-count milestone triggers (price goes up at 750, etc.)
2. "Lock in for LIFE" framing (loss aversion)
3. Countdown timers on external page
4. Post-based urgency in free community ("11 hours left")
5. "Bad news" framing when price increase happens (FOMO for those who missed it)
6. Periodic flash sales (35% off annual)

## 10. TAKEAWAYS FOR RETELL / BUILDSBYBEN COMMUNITY

1. **Lead with tangible outputs, not features.** "100+ templates ready to plug and play" beats "access to our template library."
2. **Three specific use cases > vague promise.** Marketing agents, personal assistant, faceless shorts -- each is a concrete visual.
3. **Price lock is a retention superpower.** Once someone has a locked rate, cancellation feels like burning money.
4. **Free community = distribution moat.** 239K free members is an asset that compounds. The paid tier is the monetization layer.
5. **Success stories are the best sales copy.** "Christian signed his first client in 7 days" is more compelling than any feature list.
6. **Segment your audience on the landing page.** Beginners / Business Owners / Agencies -- each sees themselves.
7. **Annual plan should gatekeep premium content.** Creates commitment + better LTV.
8. **External landing page for cold traffic.** Don't rely on Skool's about page for conversion -- build a purpose-built page.
